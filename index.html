Meet Founder

Abigail Michael, Esq.

Abigail Michael is a qualified attorney admitted to the Nigerian Bar who possesses extensive legal and mental knowledge. She also has soft skills in human relations, listening, communication, and international therapy qualifications. She started off by developing a free safe space platform for teenagers, which she then expanded and made available to everyone in 2022 as a franchise. She is a go-getter who is really enthusiastic about leading her team of counselors.
Abigail's unique blend of legal expertise and therapeutic skills allows her to provide a holistic approach to problem-solving and conflict resolution. Her goal is to empower her clients with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate challenging situations with confidence and grace. Whether you are a teenager struggling with peer pressure, or an adult dealing with emotional issues, Abigail is committed to helping you achieve your goals and live your best life.

Frequently Ask Questions

Can I pay for a session in installments?

Yes, you can pay for a session in installments. However, you must pay for the session before the session starts.

What is the refund policy?

There is no refund policy. However, you can reschedule your session if you are unable to attend the session.

Can I stay anonymous?

Definetly, we respect your privacy and we will never disclose your identity to anyone.

Can prices be negotiated?

When multiple sessions are booked, it is possible to offer a discount.

Where will the sessions hold?

All sessions are held online via zoom. You can also choose to meet in person if you are in Ife or Kwara.

How will I communicate with my therapist?

You will communicate with your therapist via email or phone call. You can also communicate with your therapist via zoom.

How long are the sessions?

Sessions are typically 50-60 minutes long. However, the exact length of a session may vary depending on your needs and goals.

I'm also a therapist. Can I provide services using your platform?

Yes, you can. We are currently looking for therapists to join our team. Please send us a mail at lightvirtualhaven@gmail.com.
